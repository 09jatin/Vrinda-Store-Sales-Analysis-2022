-- Vrinda Store Sales Analysis (Excel Project)
-- Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the monthly sales performance of Vrinda Store using Excel.
The objective is to uncover trends in revenue and order volume and identify key business insights.

-- Objectives
Analyze monthly sales and order trends
Identify peak and low-performing months
Understand business performance over time
Generate actionable insights

--Tools & Skills Used

Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Data Cleaning
Data Aggregation
Basic Business Analysis

-- Dataset Details

The dataset includes:

Month
Total Revenue (Sum of Amount)
Total Orders (Count of Order ID)

-- Data Cleaning

Removed null/blank rows
Corrected inconsistent date values
Ensured numeric formatting for revenue
Removed incorrect entries

--Key Metrics

Total Months: 12
Average Monthly Revenue: ~1.7M+
Average Monthly Orders: ~2500+

--Analysis Performed

Monthly Revenue Trend
Monthly Order Trend
Peak vs Low Month Identification
Revenue vs Orders Relationship

Key Insights
Highest revenue observed in March (~1.9M)
Lowest performance seen in November (~1.6M)
Sales show a declining trend after Q1
Strong correlation between orders and revenue

--Conclusion

The store performs strongly in early months but shows a steady decline towards year-end, indicating potential seasonality or reduced customer demand.

Future Improvements

INSIGHTS--

1. Strong Q1 Performance
Jan → March shows continuous growth
March is peak month


2. Post-March Decline
Revenue drops steadily after March

Possible Reason--
Customer interest decreases
Lack of retention strategies
No strong marketing after Q1

3. Revenue Depends on Orders
Revenue trend ≈ Order trend

Possible Reason--
Pricing is stable
Growth depends on increasing order volume

4.  Weak End-of-Year Performance
Oct, Nov, Dec are lowest

Possible Reason--
Poor festive strategy 
Competition impact
Inventory or marketing issues

5. Customer Engagement Drop
Orders gradually decline over months

Possible Reason--
Customer retention problem
Need loyalty programs / re-targeting

6.  No Sudden Spikes
No abnormal increase in any month

Possible Reason--
No major campaigns or offers executed
